An educational program for children that teaches them about biodiversity, ecology, and environmental conservation through hands-on activities and games.
Education SDG15 SDG4 Volunteering Designing
Biodiversity is essential for the survival of all living organisms on the planet. However, the rapid loss of biodiversity is a major global issue. Educating children about the importance of biodiversity and the need to conserve it is essential for a sustainable future. A biodiversity education program that is designed for children can help to raise awareness and promote action towards conservation. The program can be interactive and engaging, with activities that involve children in hands-on learning. By educating children about the importance of biodiversity, we can inspire a generation of environmentally conscious citizens who will work towards protecting the planet.
We require volunteers with a passion for education and the environment to help develop and deliver the program. We also need designers to help create engaging and interactive educational materials.
